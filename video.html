Video Production

This section showcases proficiency in Adobe Premiere Pro for composing and editing short-form video narratives. The examples below, primarily in the Public Service Announcement (PSA) format, demonstrate core skills in sequencing, visual pacing, audio editing, and delivering concise, high-impact messaging for digital platforms.

PSA: Sun Safety

An instructional video designed for quick consumption on social platforms. This project relies entirely on visual sequencing, fast-paced cuts, and clear on-screen graphics to deliver actionable health tips and protective measures without requiring audio.

PSA: Post‑Covid Mental Health

A conceptual video communicating the silent, long-term impact of remote work and isolation. The edit uses deliberately slow pacing, and framing to evoke a sense of emptiness and stillness, culminating in a visually hopeful message of self-care.
