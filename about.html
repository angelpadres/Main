About the Author

Hey there! I’m Angel, a senior at Colorado State University majoring in Journalism and Media Communications, with minors in Sociology and Interdisciplinary Information Science & Technology. I also earned an Associate’s Degree in Paralegal Studies, which reflects my love for digging into details and solving problems.

Living near Las Vegas gives me a constant dose of inspiration, a vibe I channel into my photography, writing, and travel adventures. Outside of school, I have dedicated time to impactful volunteer work. Leveraging my professional background in vision retail and a passion for community outreach, I participated in several trips across the country to assist underserved communities in receiving essential vision exams and eyewear.
