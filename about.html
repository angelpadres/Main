Angelique 'Angel' Padres, a dedicated student at Colorado State University, is majoring in Journalism and Media Communications, with minors in Sociology and Interdisciplinary Information Science and Technology. She also holds an Associate’s Degree in Paralegal Studies, showcasing her diverse interests and commitment to learning.

Angel excels in optimizing processes and introducing innovative solutions. Based near Las Vegas, she draws inspiration from the city’s vibrant energy, fueling her passion for travel and lifelong learning.

Outside of her studies, Angel is committed to community service. Through volunteering, she uses her skills to positively impact others, reflecting her belief in the importance of giving back.
