About the Author

Hey there! I’m Angel, a senior at Colorado State University majoring in Journalism and Media Communications, with minors in Sociology and Interdisciplinary Information Science & Technology. I also earned an Associate’s Degree in Paralegal Studies, which reflects my love for digging into details and solving problems.

Living near Las Vegas gives me a constant dose of inspiration. I channel that vibe into my photography, writing, and the occasional travel adventure. Outside of school, I volunteer with local nonprofits, using my communication skills to help amplify community voices.
