Her academic journey is enriched by her Associate’s Degree in Paralegal Studies, reflecting her diverse interests and dedication to continuous learning.

Known as Angel by friends and peers, she is renowned for her ability to optimize processes and introduce innovative solutions to enhance efficiency. Located just outside of Las Vegas, Angel finds inspiration in the city’s dynamic atmosphere, fueling her aspirations for frequent travel and lifelong learning.

Beyond her academic pursuits, Angel is deeply committed to giving back to her community. Through volunteering, she leverages her skills to make a positive impact in the lives of others, embodying her belief in the power of service and compassion.
